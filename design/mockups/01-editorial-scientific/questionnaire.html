Methodological assessment

Seven questions about your industrial-symbiosis case. Q1 and at least one Q3 dimension are required. The remainder is optional but improves the engine output.

Guided mode active Examples and rationale are expanded by default. Read each block carefully — the seven answers determine the methodological pathway and the data-collection plan you'll receive at the end.

What are you analysing? Required

Pick the closest match. When in doubt: who is the SUBJECT of the report?

Examples & context

A — a bilateral exchange (e.g. waste heat from Plant X to Plant Y). The subject is the FLOW BETWEEN TWO FIRMS.
B — an eco-industrial park or symbiotic network with 3+ actors (e.g. Kalundborg, NISP cluster). The subject is the NETWORK as a system.
C — a public-sector policy / programme at regional or national scale. The subject is the DECISION/PROGRAMME.
D — a single firm quantifying its symbiotic contribution for ESG / CSRD. The subject is ONE FIRM.
E — periodic monitoring of an already-operating IS (annual KPI updates).

What stage is the system in?

Existing operations versus design-only changes everything downstream.
