Critical Decision Points surfaced

3 tensions, awaiting analyst attention

CDP-04 · Allocation vs. system expansion

The chosen modelling framework (attributional, ILCD A multi-actor) and the q5 flow class (substitution) jointly recommend system expansion. The analyst must document this choice in the methodological charter; switching to allocation requires an explicit override.

resolved_at: L3 · methods: [LCA, LCC] · severity: medium

CDP-07 · Capital goods amortization

Q2=D (baseline + alternatives) activates dynamic background scenarios (SSP/RCP). The amortization period for new infrastructure defaults to 20 years; verify against the host site's actual write-off policy.

resolved_at: L3 · methods: [LCC] · severity: low

CDP-12 · Sector-specific overlay (wastewater)

Q6a=wastewater_sludge_biofactories activates AWARE water scarcity characterisation and a contamination-level field in the inventory. The DCF will surface these as additional columns in the Flow Matrix tab.

resolved_at: L3 · methods: [LCA] · severity: low
