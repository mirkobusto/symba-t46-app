Assess the environmental, economic and social benefits of industrial symbiosis

A free, publicly-accessible service to evaluate symbiosis networks in the bio-based industrial ecosystem. Use it as an industrial operator, a local authority, a community representative or an end-user — the report adapts to your audience.

Start now Read the methodology

Before you start

You'll be asked seven questions about the industrial symbiosis case you're documenting. There are no wrong answers — the engine adapts the methodology to the answers you provide. You can save your progress and come back later.

You'll need:

  • A clear idea of the actors involved (a single firm, a network, a policy)
  • Information about the stage of the system (operating, design, with alternatives)
  • The flows exchanged (waste, energy, water, by-products)
  • (Optional) The economic and social dimensions you want to assess

What you'll receive

At the end of the assessment you'll get:

  • A methodological pathway (IS-01 to IS-05) with the configuration of LCA, LCC and S-LCA for your case.
  • A Data Collection File in Excel format, calibrated on your case, to share with the partners of the symbiosis for inventory data collection.
  • A stakeholder report tailored to your audience: industry, community, local authority or end-user.
