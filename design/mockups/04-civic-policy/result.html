Things to be aware of

The engine surfaced 3 Critical Decision Points. These are methodological choices that need explicit documentation.

⚠ CDP-04 — Allocation vs system expansion

The attributional modeling framework combined with q5=c (substitution) jointly recommends system expansion. You must document this choice in the methodological charter. Switching to allocation requires an explicit override.

Methods involved: LCA, LCC · Severity: Medium

⚠ CDP-07 — Capital goods amortization

Because Q2 = D (baseline + alternatives), dynamic background scenarios (SSP/RCP) are activated. The amortization period for new infrastructure defaults to 20 years — please verify against the host site's actual write-off policy.

Methods involved: LCC · Severity: Low

⚠ CDP-12 — Sector-specific overlay (wastewater)

The sector overlay "wastewater & sludge biofactories" activates AWARE water-scarcity characterisation and a contamination-level field in the inventory. These will appear as additional columns in the Flow Matrix tab of the Data Collection File.

Methods involved: LCA · Severity: Low
