# Style 05 — Fresh Circular

> "If a sustainable-startup designed a Horizon Europe app."

The warmest, most contemporary direction. Soft pastel palette (mint + cream + leaf-green + navy), rounded everything (16-24 px corners, 999 px pills), generous spacing, friendly emoji, illustration in the hero. Nunito + DM Sans for warm typographic personality.

## References
1. TerraCycle / Loop / Too Good To Go — sustainable-startup brand voice
2. Pile / Notion / Linear cards — soft shadows, rounded corners
3. EcoVadis / Ecologi — green-economy palette with hopeful tone

## Why for SYMBA
Best fit for the **community + end-user audiences** of the GA mandate. If the demo regions / municipalities want to share the URL with citizens or with non-technical stakeholders, this style won't intimidate. Less "EU project deliverable feel", more "tool you'd actually share on LinkedIn".

## Mood
Hopeful, approachable, "we can do this". Counterweight to the academic register of Style 01 and the institutional weight of Style 04.

## Palette
- Leaf `#2D8B43` (primary action)
- Leaf-deep `#1f6831` (hover, deep CTAs)
- Mint `#D4F4DD` (highlights, soft backgrounds, badges)
- Cream `#FFFAEC` (page background — warmer than pure white)
- Navy `#0F2C4D` (text, footer, contrasting "expert" CTAs)
- Pumpkin `#F59E0B` (warnings, scoring pending, sun illustration)
- Rose `#EE6C7C` (industry tab tint — soft)

## Signature elements
- 🌱 Emoji logo mark (chunky rounded-square background)
- Hand-drawn SVG hero illustration (factory → factory flow, sun, plant)
- Pill-shaped CTAs with leaf-green shadow on hover
- Soft cards (24 px radius, low-opacity shadow)
- Pastel gradient "verdict" panel ("All done — your pathway is IS-01 extended ✓")
- Mint-tinted highlights for keywords (yellow-marker effect)
- Emoji badges throughout (🌿 ENV, 💰 ECO, 👥 SOC, 🌍 etc.)
- Audience tiles with gradient backgrounds per stakeholder type

## Tradeoffs
- Pro: most welcoming style — useful for dissemination outside the consortium
- Pro: emojis = visual scanning gain (the reader sees the type at a glance)
- Pro: distinct from anything else in the SYMBA materials — stands out
- Con: less "deliverable-defensible" — could feel under-serious to an EU reviewer who expected a JRC-grade interface
- Con: emoji-heavy = locale rendering inconsistencies on older systems
- Con: needs a real illustration (the inline SVG in the hero is OK as a placeholder; production would need a proper illustrator)
