Question 01

What kind of symbiosis are you looking at?required

Pick whichever feels closest. If you're not sure, think: who is the main subject of your report?

Need examples? Tap to expand

A — bilateral exchange. Waste heat from Plant X reused by Plant Y. The "main thing" is the flow itself.
B — eco-park / network. Multiple actors sharing flows, like Kalundborg in Denmark.
C — policy / programme. A regulatory or strategic decision at regional / national scale.
D — single firm. One company reporting their symbiotic contribution for ESG.
E — monitoring. Tracking an existing IS over time (yearly KPI updates).
